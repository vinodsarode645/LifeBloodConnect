  Introduction:
      LifeBloodConnect is a web-based blood donation and emergency blood request management system designed to connect blood donors, patients, hospitals, and blood banks on a single platform.
      LifeBloodConnect is not just a software project; it is a lifesaving initiative.
      By combining technology with humanity, this system ensures that no one suffers or loses life due to lack of timely blood availability.
      The project aims to ensure timely availability of blood, reduce emergency delays, and ultimately save human lives through technology.
  Objective:
      To provide an online platform for blood donation and blood requests.
      To connect donors, patients, and blood banks on a single platform
      To enable emergency blood request handling.
      To allow donor registration and donation history tracking.
      To provide a user-friendly and secure dashboard.
      To reduce delay caused by manual blood bank processes.
      To promote social responsibility and awareness about blood donation.
    Key Features:
      Blood Donor Registration & Verification.
      Emergency Blood Request Handling.
      Donation Centers & Blood Bank Information. 
      Real-time Blood Inventory Tracking.
      User Dashboard & Profile Management.
      Donation History Tracking.
      Secure Login And Sign-up authentication with CSRF protection.
    Technology Used:
      FrontEnd-HTML,CSS,JS.
      BackEnd-Python with(Django Framework)
      Database-MySQL
