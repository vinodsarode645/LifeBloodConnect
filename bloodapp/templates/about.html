Our Story

LifeBlood Connect was founded on a simple yet powerful belief: every life matters, and one donation can save three lives.

We recognized a critical gap in the blood donation ecosystem. Finding compatible blood donors during emergencies was complicated, time-consuming, and sometimes deadly. We set out to change that.

Today, we've built a platform that connects verified donors, hospitals, and blood banks in real-time. What started as a vision is now a lifeline for thousands of people across the country.
